FAQs

What are the stages of clothing production?

Designing, fabric selection, pattern making, cutting, sewing, finishing, inspection.

What fabrics are used?

Cotton, denim, silk, satin, knit, nylon, polyester.

What are common sewing mistakes?

Wrong measurements, improper fabric choice, uneven stitching, poor-quality accessories.

What are the signs of well-made clothing?

Straight seams, uniform fabric, good fit, high-quality accessories.
