A B O U T U S

Hi everyone! I am Akerke Tamenova, the founder of Tamen.Kz.
Our atelier is dedicated to bringing high-quality fashion
to our customers, combining traditional Kazakh craftsmanship
with modern design trends. We take pride in offering stylish
and elegant clothing that reflects our rich cultural heritage.
My mission is to introduce the Kazakh brand to the world.
Each of our creations is crafted with passion and precision,
ensuring a perfect blend of comfort and sophistication.
At Tamen Atelier, we believe that fashion is not just about
clothing—it’s a way to express identity, confidence, and individuality.
Our team is committed to creating unique, high-quality pieces
that make every customer feel special. Join us on this journey as
we redefine Kazakh fashion for the global stage!
